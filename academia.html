I have studied philosophy at the University of Padua (BA) and at the University of Florence (MA). I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Barcelona, under the supervision of Dan López de Sa and Teresa Marques. I'm mainly interested in metaethics and philosophy of language, in particular in connection with disagreement and noncognitivism (and its history, especially Charles Stevenson's work).

Publications:

Pietroiusti, Giulio (forthcoming). Disagreement and Conflict: How Moral and Taste Judgements Do Not Differ. Theoria.
